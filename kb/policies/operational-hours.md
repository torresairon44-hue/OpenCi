## Policy Statement
All OpenCI field agents and operational staff must adhere to the standard operational hours and break policies defined below.

## Enforcement Scope
This policy applies to all field agents (Fieldmen), Officers in Charge (OIC), and Field Managers operating under OpenCI.

## Operational Hours
- **Standard Timeline**: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- **Total Work Duration**: 9 hours (including break)
- **Active Work Hours**: 8 hours minimum

## Break Policy
- Maximum break duration: **1 hour** (lunch break)
- Maximum continuous work without break: 4 hours
- Break start and end times must be logged in the app
- Break location is recorded via GPS

> **IMPORTANT:** "Waiting" status gaps appear as red flags in the Workforce Dashboard timeline. Supervisors review these for compliance.

## Work Schedule Rules
- **No late reporting**: Agents must check in by 8:00 AM
- **No early outs**: Agents must complete duties until 5:00 PM
- **Photo documentation**: Must be taken in real-time during field visits, not batch uploaded later
- **Location tagging**: Must be enabled at all times during shift

## Exceptions
- Emergency situations must be reported to the Area Coordinator immediately
- Schedule adjustments require prior admin approval
- Overtime must be pre-approved by the supervising manager

## Violation Handling
- First occurrence: Verbal warning from supervisor
- Repeat violations: Escalation to Area Coordinator
- Persistent non-compliance: Formal disciplinary action per company policy

> **WARNING:** Batch uploading photos after field visits or disabling GPS during work hours are compliance violations that will be flagged automatically.
