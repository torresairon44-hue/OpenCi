## Term
Supported Banks and Partners

## Definition
The complete list of banks and financial institutions integrated with the OpenCI platform for credit investigation, demand letter, and collection services.

## Context

### Full Bank List
TFS, SBF, CBS, BDO, BPI, CSB, ESQ, EWB, FHL, FUSE, HOME CREDIT, HSBC, PNB, RSB, RCBC, PSB, UBP, MANULIFE, MAYA, MBTC, MSB, TALA, AUB

### Usage
- All listed banks can use the CI module for credit assessment and client profiling
- Bank codes are assigned to each case and must be selected correctly during case creation
- The core workflow is the same across banks; some banks may display additional system alerts with extra requirements

> **IMPORTANT:** Always verify the correct bank code before generating demand letters or submitting forms. Incorrect bank assignment delays processing.
