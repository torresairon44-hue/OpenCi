## Term
OpenCI Platform

## Definition
OpenCI is a comprehensive credit investigation and workforce management platform that connects banks and financial institutions with field investigators for credit investigation, skip tracing, demand letter delivery, and collection activities.

## Context

### Core Modules
- **CI (Credit Investigation)**: Client credit profile analysis and documentation workflows
- **DL (Demand Letters)**: Automated letter generation, printing, and delivery tracking
- **SC (Skip and Collect)**: Skip tracing and collection field activities with GPS tracking
- **Tele-CI**: Remote credit investigation via phone interviews
- **Form Builder**: Custom form creation with validation, conditional logic, and file uploads
- **Workforce Management**: Field agent scheduling, monitoring, and performance tracking

### Module Workflows
- DL Admin Workflow: GENERATED → PRINTED → RELEASED TO OIC → RELEASED TO FM → VISITED → DONE/RETURNED/PULLED_OUT
- SC Activities: Driving, Skip Tracing, Touch Point, Disposition
- Live Map: Green (Live), Grey (Stale), Blue (Home/Inactive)
- Workforce Timeline: 8 AM – 5 PM standard. "Waiting" status appears as a red gap

### Operational Hours
- Standard work hours: 8 AM – 5 PM
- Break allowance: 1 hour maximum (lunch)
- Late reporting and early outs are prohibited
